### Character attributes (same for all templates)s

-Attributes have a minimum value of 1 (unless strictly specified by another trait or template)_.

1. Physical attributes:
  * Strength
  * Dexterity
  * Stamina
2. Mental attributes:
  * Intelligence
  * Wits
  * Resolve
3. Social attributes:
  * Presence
  * Manipulation
  * Composure
